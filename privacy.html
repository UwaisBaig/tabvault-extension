Privacy Policy

TabVault collects zero personal data. Everything stays in your browser.

Overview

TabVault is a Chrome extension that saves browser tabs as tasks. This privacy policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Last updated: July 2026

Data We Collect

TabVault does NOT collect any personally identifiable information. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Authentication credentials
  • Any data that leaves your device

Data Stored Locally

TabVault stores the following data locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local and chrome.storage.sync:

  • Tab titles and URLs that you choose to save as tasks
  • Optional notes you add to tasks
  • Due dates and priority tags you set on tasks
  • Task completion status
  • Your extension preferences (theme, nudge settings)

This data never leaves your browser. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

Permissions Used

storage

Saves your task list and preferences locally in your browser. No data leaves your device.

contextMenus

Adds the "Save tab as task" option to your right-click menu. This is the core feature of the extension.

notifications

Sends a weekly reminder if you have unfinished tasks. Only fires if you have enabled this feature in settings.

alarms

Schedules the weekly nudge notification. No data is involved — this only triggers a local reminder.

activeTab

Reads the title and URL of your current tab when you choose to save it as a task. This data is saved locally only.

Host permissions (all URLs)

Allows the extension to inject a small save popup on any webpage when you right-click. The extension does not read, modify, or transmit any page content.

Third Party Services

TabVault does not use any third party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external URL the extension references is Google's favicon service (google.com/s2/favicons) to display website icons next to saved tasks.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear completed tasks" button in extension settings
  • Uninstalling the extension — this permanently removes all locally stored data

Children

TabVault does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
